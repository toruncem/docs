---
title: "First time log-in"
description: "How to create an account or log-in to an existing one made by your company"
---

### Create an account

1. Navigate to [scribe.athelas.com](http://scribe.athelas.com)
2. At the very bottom, next to "Don't have an account?", click "Sign-up"
3. Enter your work email and preferred password, then click "Create Account"

### Log-in to an account made by someone else

1. Navigate to [scribe.athelas.com](http://scribe.athelas.com)
2. Enter your work email, then click "Continue"
3. For new accounts, use "Forgot Password" to reset to a preferred password.
4. Log-in using your work email and new password.
