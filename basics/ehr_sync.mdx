### Copy Paste Sync

How to Transfer Your Note to Your EHR:

Open Athelas Scribe on your device.
Open the scribe you wish to transfer to your EHR system.
Copy the note:
a. Select the "Copy" button to copy the entire note.
b. Manually select and copy the text.
Paste the note into the patient's record in your EHR.

**Tip:** Always review your note for accuracy before transferring it to your EHR or sharing it with others.
