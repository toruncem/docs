### Copy Paste Sync

How to Transfer Your Note to Your EHR:

1. Open Athelas Scribe on your device.
2. Open the scribe you wish to transfer to your EHR system.
3. Copy the note:
    a. Select the "Copy" button to copy the entire note.
    b. Manually select and copy the text.
4. Paste the note into the patient's record in your EHR.

<Tip> Always review your note for accuracy before transferring it to your EHR or sharing it with others. </Tip>
